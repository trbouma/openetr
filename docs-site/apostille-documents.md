# Apostille Documents

Apostille Documents are a future OpenETR domain workspace.

The core idea is to treat an apostilled document, notarized document, certificate, legalization bundle, or related verification artifact as a **Controlled Object** identified by digest. Signed OpenETR control records could then provide a portable control graph for provenance, issuer attestations, authority references, revocation or replacement notices, and downstream verification.

This domain will need careful mapping to apostille conventions, competent authorities, notarization workflows, registry lookups, and jurisdiction-specific recognition policy. OpenETR can provide digest-linked signed evidence, but legal recognition of the apostille or underlying document remains with the relevant authority, law, registry, or verifier.
