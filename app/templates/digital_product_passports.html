Domain Starting Points

  • Product Passport file: a PDF, image, JSON document, or other canonical Product Passport artifact becomes the Controlled Object.
  • Initial control record: identifies the Product Passport digest and can carry product metadata such as product id, manufacturer, and batch.
  • Control graph: later events can model attestations, ownership/control changes, recalls, repairs, recycling, compliance checks, or registry recognition.
